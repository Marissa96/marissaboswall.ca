The Purpouse

I was given free-rain with this project. I could do whatever I wanted but the end result had to showcase custom photography, type and be 8 x 10.

My Solution

Everyone was doing glamour shots, so me wanting to be different, decided to show case my make-up skills. I had previously done Comic Book make-up on myself for Halloween, and loved it.

Four hours (give or take an hour) later, my model was ready. Ya, I sat there for four hours painting her face, neck and chest; drawing those stupid little dots. I used a solid background to keep the focus on the model and my amazing make-up skills.
