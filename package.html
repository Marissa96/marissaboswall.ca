The Purpose

This project was focused around picking a pre-existing package and re-designing it to make it better. The only requirement was that we had to use all the content from the old packaging on our new packaging.

The problem was picking which pre-existing package to re-design.

My Solution

After awhile of searching, I came across a product I actually had. The Bop is a little Bluetooth speaker with a suction cup on the bottom. The original packaging was just the product covered with plastic and a little card insert saying what it is and what it does.

The Bop comes with a cord so the consumer can recharge it. It also comes in various colours.

When designing the packaging, I kept in mind how it would be shipped and stacked on the shelves. The Bop goes on the bottom while the cord goes on top, forming a pyramid. The colour on the outside of the box indicates the colour of the product on the inside.
