The Purpose

For this project, we were given photos and asked to create a cosmetic billboard for our choice of company. The requirements were specific sizes and we had to edit the model photos ourselves.

My Solution

Personally, I’m the type of girl that hates wearing a lot of makeup so I chose to advertise simple cover up for my billboard. I chose Revlon because they like doing things (like how they place their word mark on a page) differently. I also chose to feature their long lasting cover up because I’m a HUGE fan of long lasting makeup (less work for me having to re-apply all the time). Since I’m the type of person that doesn’t wear a lot of makeup, I Photoshopped the model to be as natural as possible. I took off the ugly lip liner she was wearing and enhanced her eyes. I enhanced the eyes because the eyes are the first things people tend to notice about another person; also because I love eyes.

The background is used to emphasis the long lasting part of the makeup and to give the billboard some depth.
